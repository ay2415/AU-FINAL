Events

Machine Learning & Data Analytics
The Department of Computer Science and Engineering-Data Science, collaborates with Aptivex solution PVT LTD under Datanova to present a guest lecture on machine learning and Data Analytics on 10 th February in CSE Seminar Hall at Main Academic block for the 2nd year CSE-DS students to enhanced their skills in machine learning and Data Analytics.

Seminar on Career Opportunities in VLSI
Department of ECE is Seminar on Career Opportunities in VLSI on 31st January, 2024 at ECE seminar Hall, 5th Floor, LIET.

Inauguration of CSI Student Chapter
Department of Information Technology is inaugurating CSI Student Chapter on 24th January 2024 at CSE seminar Hall, II Floor, LIET.

Guest Lecture on DAA
Computer Science Department is conducting Guest Lecture on DAA-NP Complete and NP-Hard on 20-01-2024 by Mr.Abdul Bari from Udemy Academy

Blood Donation Camp
The National Service Scheme (NSS) of annamalai Institute of Engineering and Technology has organized an UPSC [Civil Services] Awareness Session in association with Civil Services Academy, Hyderabad for B.E / B.Tech / M.E./ M. Tech/ M.B.A. Students on 20th January, 2024 at Seminar Hall, 3rd floor, First Year Block (LIET).

Blood Donation Camp
The National Service Scheme Cell of annamalai Institute of Engineering and Technology(A) is organizinf Blood Donation Camp for Thalassemia Patients in collaboration with Thalassemia and Sickle Cell Society- Hyderabad. The Camp will take place on 17th January, 2024, at the 4th-floor Seminar Hall of LIET, from 10:00 am to 4:00 pm.

SDP on Communication Skills for success
The Department of Science and Humanities in collaboration with the NSS unit of annamalai Institute of Engineering and Technology, has organized a one week Student Development Programme titled “Communication Skills for Success” for B.E. 1st-year students. This SDP aims to enrich communication abilities by employing various engaging activities throughout the sessions. The program commences from 8th January 2024, at the Seminar Hall situated on the 3rd floor of the First Year Block, scheduled from 1:30 pm to 4:30 pm. Undoubtedly, this initiative will significantly benefit the participants, fostering their academic and professional endeavors.

A three day National Level Workshop on Node JS
The Department of CSE-Data Science collaborates with WebileApps under Datanova to present a three-day national-level workshop on Node.JS. Join us from January 4th to 6th at the Seminar Hall on the 2nd floor of the CSE building for an immersive learning experience.
